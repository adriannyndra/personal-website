by Belva Aprilliano A

Cats are really cute, everyone love cats, everyone wants them, until they actually get one. I've lost count how many stories i've heard of cats being used as gifts for Birthdays or as presents for loved ones or be used as a gift in general just for them to be neglected after their irresponsible owners has been bored of 'em

Not once or twice i've seen people that get out of their way to get a pet expecting a cute little fluffy company without a single thought about how prepared they are to maintain it, if the environment is good for the pet, or even if they have enough money for their essential healthcare needs etc. And once they actually get it they get struck and hit by the reality of pet care-taking, and most of the time it leads to negligence and at worse abuse.

Anger Management is also a huge deal for wannabe/future pet owners, also a must-do is to take account of your kids, and the people around us (neighbors, roommates) and anyone that might come contact to the cat can also be a huge factor.
